Gardening Zen

There is growing concern about the pollinators of this planet going extinct. They are dying off and no one understands what exactly is causing them to perish, but a few things are possible causes. We know for sure that pesticides are not helping, that the warming planet could be a contributing factor, and one thing we know for sure is that bees are suffering from a poor diet.
