Seed Savers

Seeds savers volunteers are needed all over the world

Seed saving is a skill that most of our grandparents knew and was passed down from generation to generation. People saved and shared seeds with others to maintain their gardens, because most people grew their own food. As a seed saver, you can save seeds for yourself, form relationships with seeds banks to share seeds, and also share seeds with members in your community who are also gardening to save bees.
